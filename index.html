About UNICEF

UNICEF works in over 190 countries and territories to save children's lives, to defend their rights, and to help them fulfill their potential, from early childhood through adolescence. We are the world's largest provider of vaccines for developing countries, and our efforts have helped to reduce child mortality worldwide. In addition, UNICEF provides assistance in areas such as education, nutrition, water and sanitation, and child protection.

Programs

  • Child protection and inclusion
  • Education
  • Health and nutrition
  • HIV/AIDS
  • Water, sanitation, and hygiene
  • Emergency relief
